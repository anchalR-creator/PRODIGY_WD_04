About Me

Background

The creativity and interactivity of websites online sparked my curiosity — and I was eager to build something of my own. I enjoy working on glowing-themed web pages and experimenting with visually engaging layouts, features like clipboards, and modern styling. Front-end development feels like a natural fit for me, as it allows me to blend both creativity and code.

Along the way, I've faced challenges like debugging complex errors and fixing tricky layout issues — but each hurdle has made me more confident and capable. I get excited by the idea of building professional websites, especially those for businesses or individuals who want their digital presence to stand out.

Education

I’m currently a BCA student at Presidency University, Bangalore. My passion for coding began during my PUC at Christ Junior College, where I was introduced to programming and web development in 11th and 12th grade.

Professional Experience

As a student developer, I’ve built a strong foundation in front-end development through personal projects and continuous self-learning. While I’m still gaining formal industry experience, I’ve developed skills that go beyond coding — like creative thinking, problem-solving, and adapting quickly to new technologies.

My peers and teachers often recognize me for my leadership abilities, patience, and openness to learning. Whether it’s collaborating on academic projects or helping others debug, I strive to bring a thoughtful and positive approach to every task.

I'm currently exploring opportunities to apply my skills in real-world settings, such as internships and freelance projects, to continue growing as a web developer and team contributor.
